# FolioFrame IP License Transfer Checklist

## Purpose

This checklist supports diligence for intellectual property, licensing and transfer readiness.

It is not legal advice and should be reviewed by qualified counsel before closing.

## Brand Name

- Confirm FolioFrame name ownership.
- Confirm trademark availability in relevant jurisdictions.
- Confirm whether any trademark filings exist or are needed.
- Confirm domain ownership and transfer process.

## Approved Brand Assets

Confirm transfer rights for:

- Approved primary logo files.
- Approved standalone emblem.
- Approved flagship visuals 01-10.
- Curated motif files.
- Website and portal design implementation.

## Repository Work Product

Confirm ownership or assignment for:

- Source code.
- Documentation.
- Module content.
- Pricing and offer copy.
- QA reports.
- Sale-room documents.
- Closing-package documents.

## Third-Party Dependencies

Review licenses for:

- Next.js
- React
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- Prisma
- Clerk
- pg
- ESLint
- ReportLab if PDF generation scripts are retained

## Asset Restrictions

Do not use:

- Rejected generated motifs.
- Image-generated logo variants.
- Redrawn or traced FolioFrame logos.
- Generated closing-package page images as final sale materials.
- Any asset without confirmed usage rights.

## Data And Privacy

Confirm the repository does not include:

- Real customer data.
- Production payment data.
- Live support tickets.
- Production credentials.
- Private API keys.

## Buyer Counsel Review

Before closing, buyer should review:

- IP assignment language.
- Domain transfer agreement.
- Trademark status.
- Third-party dependency obligations.
- Asset license evidence.
- Privacy policy and terms before public commercial use.

## Closing Status

Recommended status:

- Transferable as a source-code, documentation, brand-system and static demo asset.
- Not cleared as a live SaaS operating business without separate production, legal and security review.
